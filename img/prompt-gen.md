# DALL-E Prompt

## Version 1

A 2D animation logo with a square aspect ratio featuring a central imagery of a stylized CT scanner, with the outline of the machine forming a circular shape, shown from the enface view. Overlaid in front of the CT scanner is an imaginary fountain pen. The CT scanner uses a palette of cool tones, including blues, grays, and whites. The fountain pen has a vibrant color palette with shades of orange and purple. The animation should have a smooth and modern design, suitable for a professional app logo.
